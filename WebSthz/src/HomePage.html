Here is our story:

Welcome to Drone Dad, where innovation takes flight. Established on October 31, 2015, Drone Dad has rapidly emerged as a main force inside the dynamic international of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), dedicated to crafting drones that empower people. Our journey began with a shared imaginative set of passionate technologists, inspired by their love of drones and flying. Led via our founder, Bobert Macklethorpe, a skilled engineer with a penchant for innovation, Drone Dad got down to redefining the possibilities of UAV generation. Since our inception, Drone Dad has been devoted to pushing the boundaries of drone layout and functionality. From our flagship product, the "Ducky Drone," famed for its superior duckabilities and ducky-inspecting camera, to our cutting-edge advancements in independent flight and aerial images, every Drone Dad drone is meticulously crafted to deliver unparalleled performance. What sets Drone Dad apart is our unwavering dedication to our center values: innovation and integrity. With a numerous crew of engineers, designers, and visionaries on the helm, we aim to push the boundaries of what is possible in the UAV industry, all even as staying true to our challenge of empowering. Thank you for joining us on this outstanding journey. Together, let's fly to new heights with Drone Dad.
